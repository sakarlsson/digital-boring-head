# digital boring head


## Adjusting the boring radius

1 mm of radius change per revolution off the adjusting dial.

The Motor has 210.591: in gearing .

Ther will 631.7719 pulses per rev.


## Components

* Arduino Nano IOT 33 

* A DRV8833 H-bridge (Amazon:  B08KHMZMP3) 

* 9V Battery 

* 3D printed housing. 

### Motor

Verry small DC motor with gearbox and encoder. 

Specifications:

    Operating voltage: 12 V
    Gear ratio: 210.591:1
    Maximum torque: 0.353 Nm (50 oz-in)
    Speed: 130 rpm
    Current consumption (no load): 70 mA
    Current consumption (maximum load): 1600 mA
    Cable length: 100 mm

Encoder:

    Pulses per revolution (after gearbox): 631.7719
    Pulses per revolution (before gearbox): 12

Dimensions:

    Motor: 40.5 × 12 × 10 mm
    Shaft: Ø3 mm × 10 mm (D-shaped)
    Weight: 13 g
